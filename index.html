Mystic Esports Official

Be mystic

Mystic Esports Official is an Esports organisation registered under AKGPA. We are mainly focusing in PUBG Mobile (Global) and Battlegrounds Mobile India. We strongly believe that the one who has a deep passion and with much dedication anyone can be a pro. We always value their effort. And also we are ready to provide guidance and make a strong line-up at anytime. If anyone needs to improve their skills and reflex, then this is an open chance that you are welcome to our family.

ABOUT

Mystic is a clan which is formed in our local circle for PUBGMOBILE (GLOBAL). Later we decided to build it as an Esports Org. On November 2019, we have started our Esports journey and our main line-ups are actively participating in Local Room matches. On July 2020, our org has got registered to AKPGA membership under AKEF. We have also helped our fellow-mates to improve their skills and we have succeeded in doing so. Now we are continuing Esports Org at global level and as well as in national level.
